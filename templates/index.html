Mode Comparison: Fresh Air vs Recirculation

💨 Fresh Air Mode

✓ Pros

  • Reduces CO2 buildup
  • Improves oxygen levels
  • Clears odors
  • Filter removes PM2.5

✗ Cons

  • Exposes to outdoor pollutants
  • Increases filter wear
  • Higher fuel consumption
  • Less efficient in traffic

♻️ Recirculation Mode

✓ Pros

  • Blocks outdoor pollution
  • Preserves filter life
  • Better fuel efficiency
  • Protects in smog

✗ Cons

  • CO2 builds up
  • Reduced oxygen
  • Window fogging risk
  • Odor accumulation
